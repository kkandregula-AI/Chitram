Prompting that works

Images: subject → setting → light → lens → style, in that order. Name the shot ("35mm", "macro", "overhead flat-lay"). For text in images, use NB Pro and put the exact words in quotes.

Video: describe one continuous camera move ("slow dolly in", "orbit right"), one subject action, and the mood of sound. Veo reads film grammar fluently.

Model IDs evolve — if Google renames one, update it in Settings → Model IDs without touching code.
